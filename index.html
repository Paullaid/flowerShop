Did you Know

Our mission is to drive progress and enhance the lives of our customers by delivering superior products and services that exceed expectations.

Rose

Classic and romantic, roses come in many colors, last about a week, and are perfect for love and special occasions.

Tulip

Simple yet elegant, tulips symbolize spring and renewal, with stems that bend toward light and last 5-7 days.

Lily

Striking and fragrant, lilies (like Asiatic or Oriental) add drama to bouquets and can last up to 10 days.

Sunflower

Cheerful and bold, sunflowers bring warmth to any bouquet but need frequent watering to stay fresh.

Carnation

Affordable and long-lasting (up to 2 weeks), carnations come in bright colors and are great for festive arrangements.

Peony

Luxuriously fluffy and fragrant, peonies are a wedding favorite but are only available in late spring and early summer.
